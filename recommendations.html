Other stuff I like

There's lots of very interesting people on this planet, but it can be hard to find them amongst all the background noise.
Although most of these podcasts are available on Spotify I have issues with them being 'exclusives' to a single platform, as this can make the hosts beholden to the platform. Therefore I've not put links in, if you already listen to podcasts you should be able to add these to your player of choice pretty easily. I personally use Pocket Casts on my android phone.

Podcasts:

Podcast are a great source of information, and I enjoy hearing about the world from different points of view. I usually listen slightly sped up (x1.1 speed) as this doesn't stop me taking in the information but really makes a difference whne listening to particularly long episodes. I listen to a combination of tech podcasts, news podcasts and then a few about my other interests.
